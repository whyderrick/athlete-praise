About the Admirer

Derrick is a writer and technologist who loves to think about public opinion and the people who influence it. He grew up in highly competitive sporting environments, and watching the 90s-era Bulls.

A few of his favorite other favorite activities and considerations are:

  • Video games and comics as a means of analyzing speculative fiction
  • Poetry & Performance
  • The position of hip-hop in an ever-changing America
  • Perspective and the relativity of experience

Marshawn Lynch

(also known as Beast Mode)

Who’s Marshawn?

Marshawn Lynch is an NFL running back who played with the Buffalo Bills and Seattle Seahawks. He is a native of Oakland California and is extremely reticent in interview situations.

In addition to being something of a nightmare for NFL journalists, he's a terror for his opponents as well. He’s known as “Beast Mode” for his tendency to run through would-be tacklers as a boar might run through a hunter.
